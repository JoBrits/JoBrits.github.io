FNB Turquoise specific guidelines

FNB Turquoise can be used as a background colour. When it is, all text including our legal details must be white
Can be used for text on a white background
Can be used as the fill colour for shapes
Can be used in a llillustrative elements(icon, pictogram, infographic, illustration)
Can not be used for type over photographic images unless the area where it is to appear is white or extremely light (requires BI team approval)
Should not be used for small text due to legibility issues

Guidelines applicable to tints and shades of turquoise

Tints or shades of turquoise may be used in infographics, storytelling and feature illustrations
May not be used for text
May not be used for backgrounds
In certain circumstances a tint or shade of turquoise may be used for a text symbol or coloured panel (requires BI team approval)
