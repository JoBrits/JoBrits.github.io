Verify Account Owner

Verify the ownership of a new recipient's account before adding and verify before changing account details of an existing creditor. Once the ownership of a bank account is verified, you can securely effect any transactions to the recipients knowing the ownership of the account has been independently verified as correct. This easy option can be used to ensure additional peace of mind by screening all your existing and future recipients banking details before using them to make payments online.

All you need is the account number and ID number of the person you're paying. If it's a business, you'll need the account number, business name and business registration number. It also allows you to verify some other fields, like their cellphone number or email address.

We strongly recommend that you verify banking details of all new suppliers/creditors, and to check on any changes made to bank accounts belonging to existing suppliers/creditors – just to make sure all information is correct and authentic.
