3rd Party Investment Manager

3rd Party Investment Manager is an online Third Party Funds Management system which allows 3rd Party Investment Managers such as Attorneys, Property Managers, Estate Administrators, Debt Collectors and Estate Agents to manage entrusted funds on behalf of their clients on Online Banking Enterprise™.
In order to use the 3rd Party Investment Manager functionality via Online Banking Enterprise™, a Trust Account needs to be opened and linked to an Investment Structure on FNB's back office systems, this can by your Relationship Manager. Once opened, you can operate the 3rd Party Investment Manager system completely independently and require no input, or assistance from a FNB bank representative.

This functionality enables the 3rd Party Investment Manager to perform the following functionality via Online Banking Enterprise™:

  • Create and Maintain of 3rd Party Client profiles
  • Open Investment (Notice, Call and Fixed) Accounts on behalf of 3rd Party Clients
  • Perform various Instructions on the 3rd Party Clients Investment Accounts (Notice to withdraw, maturity withdrawal and reinvestment)
  • Perform Financial Transactions (Fund, Transfers, Payments) on Investment Accounts
  • Close Investment Accounts
  • View and extract the required Statements and Reports on the Trust and Investment Accounts

Property Guarantee

Our Property Guarantee functionality on the 3rd Party Investment Manager (3PIM) system allows an attorney to issue cash backed property guarantees.

The functionality is specifically built for Attorney’s who hold a valid Fidelity Fund Certificate, as well as top-up Professional Indemnity cover. This enables attorneys to issue Property Guarantees at no cost. You have full control of the system and are empowered to instantly issue, cancel and pay Guarantees without any manual intervention from FNB. You have the ability to issue multiple Guarantees against an individual investment account and only pledge the portion of money required for each specific guarantee.
