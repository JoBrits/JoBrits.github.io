Control your finances from anywhere

The good news is that you still enjoy seamless banking with FNB while overseas. Remember - you must let us know that you will be out of the country, and you can do this via Secure Chat on the FNB Banking App.

 
What you can do

Purchase data in the country you are visiting, find all our important contact numbers on the FNB Banking App, keep track of your account activity just as you would at home and still report fraud immediately

 
How to get it

Download the FNB Banking App and link your device before you leave.
