Authenticate and Auhorise your Online Banking Enterprise™

Transacting with Smart InContact for Business we offer multiple web and mobile security features and controls that can be adapted to suit your needs. The multi-layered approach allows you to adopt the security features and controls around your own specific business security needs.

Smart inContact for Business is our recommended two factor authentication mechanism

It uses the Banking App to both authenticate you securely and allow authorisation of your Online Banking Enterprise™ transacting.

  • If a User logs in to their Online Banking Enterprise™ profile from a new and/or unverified browser, or from a different PC, the User will be prompted to confirm this request via the FNB Banking App using their unique PIN ; and
  • A Notification to authorise all transactions, will be delivered to your authorised User's linked and paired FNB Banking App.

The notification will prompt the User to Authorise the transaction or to Report Fraud immediately. Smart inContact for Business eliminates the risks linked to SIM Swap fraud and mitigates the risks introduced by malware, until this is removed by your security software.

Authorising your Transactions:

1
Authorise transactions
When you request authorisation on your PC, the screen will display a unique ID, which will also appear in the notification sent to your linked device.
We deliver a notification directly to your phone requesting you to 'Approve' or 'Decline' the transaction, or 'Report fraud' if you are not responsible for the transaction.
2
Verify the unique ID
Verify that the unique ID in your device notification matches the one detailed on your PC screen.
3
Authorise or report fraud
Enter your unique 4-digit PIN to complete the transaction or report fraud immediately.

You need to be vigilant.

Check the details of every transaction or device that you are Authorising or Authenticating.

Always check your notification messages to ensure that the notification you are approving is for the transaction you intend to perform. Verify that all device notifications sent via SMS or via push messages on the Banking App, matches the transactions or instructions you are performing.

If it’s not you accessing your banking profile or initiating a linking request, please decline it and report fraud immediately.

Profile Administratation
We recommend appointing at least two system administrators to create and maintain crucial user and site settings.
Online Banking Enterprise™ Administrators determine how your online transacting is set up on every level, and is split into two distinctive levels namely site and user permissions. It's really the foundation of your online banking security and the effectiveness of this is further enhanced if more than one Administrator is required to make these decisions.
Dual Authorisation on all transacting
The authorisation function provides you with a tangible control to enforce segregation of duties between capturers and authorisers.
It's important that you know that singular levels of authority (that is, only one person authorising payments) comes with some risks, increased possibility of errors and even fraud. Because of this, we really recommend Dual Authorisation on all forms of payments as a minimum control.
Make sure your devices are clean
Use adequate protection, we also offer free Trend Micro Anti-Virus and Anti-Malware software for registered users for your PC and Smartphone.
Keep your software updated and uninstall and Apps you are no longer using. We recommend that you always use the latest Software, Browsers and/or Apps on all your PC's and Smartphone devices to ensure that you get the benefits of all our latest functionality as well as the most up-to-date online security.
Control Access to your Online Profile
Manage your users access with date and time access lockdown restrictions.
This mitigates risks surrounding any unauthorised use of your Online Profile outside of your usual operating hours, or over the weekend and / or Public Holidays. These lockdown settings will also determine when users will be able to access using either their Tablet or Smartphone if these are authorised for use.

Your security whilst transacting within the digital environment is important to us

and it is imperative that you are aware of the threats that exist, in order to ensure you do not fall victum to these.

  • NEVER accept a request to link a new device unless you initiated the link from your new device yourself.
  • NEVER click on links – we will never send you an email requesting you to complete your personal details by selecting a link in an email.
  • NEVER give information out any personal information, regardless of the information the requestor may already have about you.
  • NEVER share your username and password with anyone.
